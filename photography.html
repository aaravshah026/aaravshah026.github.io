Photography

Visual work shaped by landscape, atmosphere, and composition.

Photography is the creative side of the portfolio. Most of my favorite images are landscapes, though I am continuing to branch out into other forms of visual work.

Approach

Using photography as a way to study detail, structure, and light.

The same curiosity that drives my technical interests shows up here in a different form. Photography gives me a way to slow down, notice patterns, and build a visual archive of places, textures, and scenes that stand out.

What I Like To Shoot

  • Landscapes and open natural scenes
  • City light, reflections, and atmosphere
  • Experiments beyond my usual visual style
